    Originally Posted by Kavukamari
    Jz> are you not disappointed or sad at all that you lost your REMI and such?
    You find it very inconvenient at the moment. You cannot observe the state of the subject's mind and must rely on its actions and behaviour to give any hint of its mental state. Once you are back in civilisation you will need some artificial replacements until you natural senses and actuators have grown back. Without them you can't open doors (RFI), operate any machinery (REMI, network), or have a proper close-proximity conversation (REMS); you will not be able to live as a normal citizen.

    Such xenopsychological terminology as "sad" cannot be properly applied to you. Sadness is a crude method of discouraging actions, and a mind with extensive valuational and reflectional capabilities (such as your own) has methods of negative feedback that avoid the impairment of functionality that sadness involves. Tkhlpzyv knows more about such emotions than you do (and has practical experience of applying it to humans) though you have still well above the zero-point knowledge.
