They have tracked the subject by its distinctive temperature; the metabolism needed for its (intended) mental and physical capabilities gives it the highest body temperature of any large creature around: 320K. Currently it is in some woodland at the north edge of the area that the humans have colonised. The woodland obstructs the view, but this area it must be within is quite small, so a ground search would be practical.

However, you have been looking at data from the probes on the way over here, and you have found that this area presents an opportunity to interact with humans that have not encountered civilised members of your species for a long time.

If the subject has formed friendships with the humans around it, then befriending them will cause the subject to trust you more. OTOH, if the subject has antagonised the humans around it, then opposing the humans will win its trust more rapidly.
