The citizen next to you is Uinojwdk. Being in such close proximity to one another for an extended period of time offers an opportunity for exploring and practising manipulation of each others brains. Such activities help ensure the security of civilisation because individuals with defective minds (especially those with defective motivation) are found rapidly; indeed without it there could not be enough trust established for true co-operation to occur. Also, it is good practice of using REMS and REMI, and reflective capabilities.

You and Uinojwdk place your foreheads as close together as is possible without moving off the acceleration couches. You both produce simplified mental states in yourselves, and sense them in each other, and attempt to re-induce those states in each other. You create firing patterns in one another's brains that do not correspond to any normally-possible thought or sensory input, and attempt to manipulate and re-create the patterns in yourselves.

You each suppress and induce signals in each other to create purified instances of pain, pleasure, other more complex moral drivers, the empathetic versions of them, the anticipated versions of them, etc., including ones with several levels of recursion and composition. As you experience each, you remember it and use it to calibrate your own motivational system, and observe that the other has done so correctly.

Uinojwdk is rather better than you at all that, but you can detect yourself improving, and your potential is certainly greater than someone of Ui's design; your disadvantage is being young. However, Ui does not have the skill that Vcypsua has gained from years of study of mental states and practice at exploring, manipulating, and repairing the minds of feral individuals. Neither does Ui have the unusual style of Tkhlpzyv, but Tk is rather a special case due to Tk's childhood. Tk and Vc of course have far more knowledge of your mind than Ui does, since they have known you for most of your life but Ui has known you for less than an hour.

Learning and adjusting yourself so hard has the side-effect of each sensation being strongly stored in memory. At the end you must de-intensify them quickly, lest you become addicted.

Most of the neurons within your skull are now tired, so you put them in a low-activity state for a while to recover. Other parts of your brain will re-activate them when necessary or after a time. They will have recovered long before this capsule reaches the space station.
