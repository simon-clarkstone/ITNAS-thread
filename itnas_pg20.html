    As the ship approaches from the north (and you head north) you both see the source of the sound that you originally heard to the west of the hole you were in, but had not seen before this point.

    ---



    You wake up. Your telekinesis nodes and REMS/REMI ridges have all been removed.

    You have suffered damage around the area where your aerial connects to your brain. You cannot detect any radio transmissions at all, not even the background noise or your own voice (which would be disabled anyway by the same damage). You must have gotten EMPed.

    Your cybernetics detected that your lower mind was deactivated for a while just after you became unconscious. That wouldn't be a consequence of EMP, but must have been done quite deliberately, since if done accidentally you would have stopped breathing and died from it. You take a moment to restore your sense of time from the clock. Your energy levels and the taste of the little food that remains in your gut match what you expect given the time that clock says.

    Also you cannot see anything, but that is just because you are blindfolded as well as bound.
