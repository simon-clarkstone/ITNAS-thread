    I'd say it's not actually a plotless mess - but the fact that it's being told at a fairly slow pace means that the plot you have is being portioned out in very small chunks that, ideally, would either be given faster (Andrew Hussie and the eighty-updates-in-seven-days update schedule may be a bit extreme, and I believe you also have a day job? but faster than the present pace would be ideal) or covered in larger sections (the previous comments about a lack of backstory are also fairly true).

    I'd say, if you don't wish to do a recap all at once (which you probably don't), at least expand the descriptions and/or dialogue in proportion to the number of images per update, and provide occasional reminders of previous events in order to clarify the context of the current panel and provide more backstory more quickly. This should mitigate said problem.
